The brand

Parisianist is a French ready-to-wear brand for men and women founded by Rare Chandelier. The founder and artistic director draws his inspiration from the 3 main artistic fields of fashion, art and music and intends to convey a movement which offers a collaboration in perfect harmony of these 3 fields. As a result, he aims to collaborate with new wave talents from different artistic backgrounds (architects, musicians, dancers, actors, directors, painters, sculptors, etc.)

The name

The name Parisianist comes from the term “Parisianism” which initially designates linguistic and cultural traits specific to Parisians. At the end of the 16th century, it designated a “way of speaking specific to Parisians” although it has very recently taken on a pejorative connotation designating a form of Parisian ethnocentrism. The brand DNA focuses on the idea of being a major player in the promotion of French aesthetics and know-how internationally Finally, the name Parisianist can also be presented as a religion based on the charm of Parisian culture.

The aesthetic

The brand is intentionally minimalist, the cuts are refined and creative to offer an elegant silhouette. Inspired by the rigor and aesthetics of haute couture houses, it offers a timeless, sophisticated Parisian style and is made with materials selected for their quality and designed to be worn every day and for special occasions. Each campaign must convey a poetic, elegant and artistic aesthetic.

The inspiration

The brand mainly draws its influences from the fields of fashion, art and music. The style of Parisian icon Carine Roitfeld is a major source of inspiration, but also that of actress Audrey Hepburn in her most iconic films. The brand is an admirer of French chic popularized by brands such as Chanel or Saint Laurent. In addition, Parisianist adores the elegant photography of photographer Guy Bourdin. Finally, she draws her inspiration from Parisian architecture, museums and 5-star hotels.

The goal

The brand aims to be a showcase of French aesthetics and know-how internationally and aims to collaborate with major French luxury brands. Its aim is to become essential in the positioning of luxury ready-to-wear in order to join the pantheon of haute couture houses, which influenced it, on avenue Montaigne.
